Who are we?

A modern payment aggregator with the most popular services

The company was established in 2017, bringing together an experienced team of programmers and financial specialists. VessPay has undergone two upgrades of technical parts of the website and now can provide modern and high-quality services.

What do we do?

VessPay offers different services, such as salary project for commercial grantors, fiat- and cryptocurrencies exchange, and payments for different services and subscriptions.

You can use the services of the VessPay at any time, as well as get an online chat consultation from the operator.

How do we do it?

VessPay carries out all operations automatically with the Telegram bot’s help.

To carry out any service you just have to start the Telegram bot and choose the service you need.

Why do we do it?

Our goal is to create high-quality service, which will be useful and convenient for VessPay’s clients.

We carefully work on the technical side of our service to ensure reliability and security, and also think through every little detail in the design to ensure the convenience of using the service.
